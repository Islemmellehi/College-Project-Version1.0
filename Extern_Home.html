About Nafs

Mental health is sadly not getting the attention it deserves. Society has decided that mental health should settle down on the scale of human priorities. Which makes it really hard for people to ask for help with their mental health related problems in fear of being shamed, bullied or simply not understood. This is where Nafs steps in. Since changing society's view on mental health isn't something we can do overnight, we have decided to make it easier for you to get the help you need by helping you connect to a certified psychologist of your choice online.

Services

Certified Psychologists

Nafs provides professional help given by certified Psychologists so that you don't have to worry about the authenticity of the help you will get.

Online sessions

Nafs connects you to your psychologist online to make it easier for you to schedule sessions.

Free Articles

Nafs provides free online articles and guides to help you get educated about mental health in order to relate and feel understood and also to help you understand those around you. Articles can be written by people who wanna share their stories or by professionals who want to explain and breakdown issues.
